First Place Winner

∎

First place winner of a marketing case competition for Zip Car’s move into Calgary, hosted by the SFU Student Marketing Association in partnership with Red the Agency. After a thirteen hour work day we presented the following 3 marketing strategy proposals to a panel of judges and executives to take the gold.

No Sweat Maintenance

A marketing series focusing on Social Media and University advertising. Highlights the ease and inexpense of maintenance and winter driving with Zip Car.

Take Your Pick & Drive in Style

A marketing series focusing on the Calgary Stampede, and Social Media Advertising. Highlights the Prarie identity, and the social and status identification of driving luxury cars and pick-up trucks. Underlines the economic benefit of paying less, while still driving a nicer car.

Cars That Adjust To Your Lifestyle

A marketing series which ties together the first two ideas and expands upon them. Focuses on key 'Yuppie' demgraphic, launches at Calgary Folk Music festival, and advertises at the Universities, and at the malls. Focuses on living the Zip Car lifestyle, easier to shop, attend festivals and events, and go out with friends in luxury.
